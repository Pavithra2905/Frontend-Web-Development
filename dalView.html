Detailed View

Urad dal, also known as black gram or black lentil, is a highly nutritious and versatile legume commonly used in South Asian cuisine. This small, black-skinned lentil is packed with protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals, making it a valuable addition to a balanced diet. Urad dal is available in whole, split, and de-husked forms, each offering a unique texture and flavor profile. It is a key ingredient in a variety of traditional dishes, including dals, soups, and stews, as well as popular snacks like vadas and papadums. When cooked, urad dal has a creamy texture and a rich, earthy flavor that complements a wide range of spices and herbs. In addition to its culinary uses, urad dal is also known for its health benefits, including aiding digestion, improving heart health, and providing sustained energy. Whether used in everyday cooking or special festive recipes, urad dal is an essential pantry staple in many households, celebrated for its versatility and nutritional value.
